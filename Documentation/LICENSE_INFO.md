## Alternative Licenses Considered

### Apache License 2.0
- **Pro**: Patent protection, contributor licensing
- **Con**: More complex, longer text
- **Use case**: Larger commercial projects with patent concerns

### GNU GPL v3
- **Pro**: Ensures derivative works remain open source
- **Con**: "Copyleft" - requires derivative works to use same license
- **Use case**: When you want all derivatives to stay open source

### Creative Commons (Assets)
- **Pro**: Designed for creative works (art, music, etc.)
- **Con**: Not suitable for code
- **Use case**: For game assets, art, documentation

### Unlicense (Public Domain)
- **Pro**: No restrictions whatsoever
- **Con**: No legal protections, may not be valid in all jurisdictions
- **Use case**: When you want to give up all rights
